A little bit About Me

Always ready to take on new challenges, I have built my career across a diverse range of lean startups and established companies. I began my journey as a Creative UI/UX Executive at Lelong.my, a homegrown e-commerce platform, and quickly advanced to Lead UI/UX Designer roles at Supahands, where I shaped the design of innovative AI-driven solutions. My experience expanded further during my time at Maxis, where I contributed to a crucial MVNO project, honing my skills in creating seamless and user-friendly interfaces.


I later took on the role of Chief Project Officer at INVOKE Malaysia, an NGO focused on activism, where I was instrumental in preparing for Malaysia's 14th General Election campaign. After a 10-month sabbatical spent freelancing, mentoring budding product designers, and exploring business ideas, I became Head of Product at Maideasy, where I restructured the company's business verticals to focus on generating steady revenue.


Most recently, I ventured into the fishing and tackle industry with MR. UMPAN, diving into the complexities of entrepreneurship. This journey has only deepened my understanding of business dynamics and reinforced my passion for crafting impactful design solutions.
