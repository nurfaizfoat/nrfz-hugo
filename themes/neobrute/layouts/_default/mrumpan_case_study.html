Mr. Umpan

Project Duration

Team

1 x Product Owner

Company

Mr. Umpan

My Role

I am the proprietor of Mr. Umpan, a small business established in the first quarter of 2020. Mr. Umpan is a local fishing tackle brand offering ready-to-use freshwater fishing paste bait, fishing reel servicing grease and lube, and a pocketable fishing hook sharpener.


Background

I found myself out of the workforce as COVID-19 severely impacted Malaysia. The government's ban on outdoor activities led to my former employer, a service industry company reliant on human interaction, being unable to sustain its monthly overheads.


Meanwhile, my partner, a healthcare professional, had to continue working during the pandemic, leaving me to care for our two children, who could not attend school due to the Movement Control Order (MCO). With no other options, I began homeschooling them.


Instead of waiting for the market to recover, I decided to pursue my long-held idea of creating a tangible product. With a startup capital of MYR500, I registered the business with the ROC under the name Mister Bait Trading, marking the beginning of this entrepreneurial journey.This business is run fully online via a popular retail marketplace called Shopee.


GTM Strategy

Given the lean approach for Mr. Umpan, my marketing efforts have been centered on organically generating leads and converting them. I've utilized social media content to capture as much attention as possible, followed by strategic follow-up content to facilitate conversions.


One notable example was creating a how-to video featuring myself catching fish using our unique paste bait. This initiative was crucial in sparking curiosity among our target customers and encouraging them to try our bait.


I also conducted a thorough competitor analysis to identify pain points and addressed them by introducing distinctive packaging. I was the first to use a kraft sealed paper bag for bait packaging, complete with clear usage instructions. This superior packaging not only extended the shelf life of our bait but also protected it from outdoor elements, a significant improvement over the simple clear plastic packaging used by competitors.


This same strategy was applied when I launched the Grease series and the Pocket-able Hook Sharpener. Through extensive competitor analysis, I identified opportunities to address pain points and successfully shipped my products. This helped Mr. Umpan disrupt the market and gain a substantial share from existing players.


Moving Forward

After nearly four years of bootstrapping Mr. Umpan with an initial capital of MYR500, I've grown the business to achieve a monthly recurring revenue (MRR) of MYR3,500, though not as large as initially envisioned.


This year, I'm expanding my reach by entering the brick-and-mortar market. I plan to sell Mr. Umpan products to fishing tackle retailers through a consignment model, diversifying my sales channels beyond online presence.


Looking ahead, I intend to apply the insights gained from Mr. Umpan to other tangible product business ideas, aiming to create multiple income streams. This strategic expansion will help me build a robust and diversified business portfolio.
